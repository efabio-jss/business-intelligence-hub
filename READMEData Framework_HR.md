# 👥 HR Dashboard – Power BI Report

This project demonstrates how to build a **Human Resources (HR) data framework in Power BI**, focused on employee distribution, promotion pipeline, retrenchment planning, and operational insights.

> 🧪 This is a fictional dataset used for educational purposes and Power BI learning exercises.

---

## 📊 Overview

The dashboard offers a **full HR insight view** across 3 main pages:

### 1. 🏠 Home
- **Total Employees:** 1,470
- **Gender Distribution:** 60% Male / 40% Female
- **Promotion Readiness:** 
  - 72 employees due for promotion (4.9%)
- **Retrenchment Projection:**
  - 117 employees flagged (8%)
- **Tenure Analysis:** Employee distribution by years of service
- **Job Levels Breakdown**
- **Workforce Proximity:** Distance from office (Very Close, Close, Very Far)

### 2. 🧭 Action
- Lists employees individually:
  - **Due for Retrenchment**
  - **Due for Promotion**
- Built for HR teams to **act fast** on career planning or risk management
- Clear visual separation and totals for both categories

### 3. 🔍 Detail
- Cross-sectional views:
  - **Promotions & Retrenchments by Department**
  - **Job Satisfaction Distribution**
  - **Employees working Overtime**
  - **Rating summaries**
- Dynamic employee role table for full visibility

---

## 🛠️ Features & Techniques

- 🧩 **DAX Measures** for key KPIs
- 📎 **Slicers** and linked navigation buttons
- 📊 **Stacked bars, donut charts, KPIs, tables**
- ✅ Conditional formatting & dynamic text
- 💡 Designed to support **data-driven HR decisions**
